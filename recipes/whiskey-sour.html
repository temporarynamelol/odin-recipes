Whisky Sour

Description

The whiskey sour is a mixed drink containing whiskey (often bourbon), lemon juice, sugar, and optionally, a dash of egg white or cocktails foamer.

  • 2 teaspoons simple syrup
  • 1 teaspoon water
  • 2 dashes bitters
  • 1 cup ice cubes
  • 45ml bourbon whiskey
  • 1 slice orange
  • 1 cherry
  1. Pour simple syrup, water and bitters into whiskey glass
  2. Stir to combine
  3. Place ice cubes in glass
  4. Pour bourbon over ice
  5. Garnish with the cherry and orange slice
