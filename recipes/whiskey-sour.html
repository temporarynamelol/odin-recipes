Whisky Sour

Description

The whiskey sour is a mixed drink containing whiskey (often bourbon), lemon juice, sugar, and optionally, a dash of egg white or cocktails foamer.

Ingredients

  • 60ml bourbon
  • 20ml lemon juice, freshly squeezed
  • 15ml simple syrup
  • 15ml egg white (optional)
  • Garnish: Angostura bitters

Instructions

  1. Add bourbon, lemon juice, simple syrup and egg white, if using, to a shaker and dry-shake for 30 seconds without ice.
  2. Add ice and shake again until well-chilled.
  3. Strain into a coupe glass.
  4. Garnish with 3 or 4 drops of Angostura bitters.
